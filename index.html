About

Card Game is a simple, flexible platform for prompt-based conversation games. Each game is defined entirely in games.json — swap the contents to create a completely different experience.

Designed to bring people together around meaningful questions, playful prompts, or creative challenges. No points, no winners — just good conversation.

How it works: each player takes turns clicking a deck to reveal a prompt card. Read it aloud, answer honestly, and listen to everyone else's response before moving on.

To add your own game, open games.json and add a new entry with a unique gamename, a title, a colour theme, and three decks of prompts. Then link to it with ?q=yourgamename.
